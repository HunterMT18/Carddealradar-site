About Card Deal Radar

Card Deal Radar is a deal discovery service built for trading card collectors. Our system scans the hobby to identify undervalued listings, hidden auctions, bulk lots, and card supply deals across online marketplaces.

Members receive alerts when opportunities are discovered so they can review potential deals before the broader market notices them.

What Our Radar Looks For

Hidden Listings

Mispriced or overlooked cards that may be undervalued compared to recent sales.

Auction Opportunities

Auctions ending soon that may still be priced below market value.

Bulk Card Lots

Large card lots that may contain cards worth significantly more individually.

Supply Deals

Discounts on sleeves, top loaders, magnetic holders, and storage supplies.

How It Works

  1. Our radar scans marketplaces for interesting listings.
  2. Potential deals are reviewed before alerts are shared.
  3. Alerts are posted to the Card Deal Radar community.
  4. Members review the listing and decide if the opportunity is worth pursuing.
